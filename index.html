Controls

  • Arrows (←)(→) to rotate left and right
  • Arrows (↑)(↓) to move camera closer or further
  • Keys (U)(I) to tilt camera up or down
  • Keys (Q)(W) to move couches
  • Keys (A)(S) to move chairs
  • Key (O) to open or close the Door
  • Key (x) to switch sun ON or OFF
  • Space Key to switch TV ON or OFF
